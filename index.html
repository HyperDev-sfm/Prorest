🧠 Prorest

A Reinforcement Learning Rocket League AI by Prolest

About

Prorest is an AI trained using reinforcement learning to play Rocket League through self-play and curriculum learning.

Status

🟡 Currently in active training (Fundamentals stage)

Training Setup

  • RLGym
  • GigaLearner-PPO
  • 115 parallel environments
  • Custom reward functions

Availability

Prorest is private and not publicly downloadable.
